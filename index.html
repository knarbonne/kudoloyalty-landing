How it works

Live in under 15 minutes

No developers, no integrations, no headaches. Follow the guided setup and you're ready to go.

1

Set up your program

Pick your loyalty mode — points, stamps, or online claims — and add your first rewards. The guided wizard walks you through LINE connection and branding step by step.

2

Share with customers

Customers join through LINE — the app they already use every day. No separate download, no new account, no friction.

3

Staff confirms in-store

When a customer redeems a reward, your staff confirms via the admin app on any device. Or issue instant digital coupons — your choice.

4

Engage and broadcast

Automatically group members by activity level. Send targeted LINE messages to re-engage at-risk members or reward your most loyal ones.

Segments & broadcasts

Automatically group members by activity (New, Active, At risk, Lapsed) or create custom segments. Send targeted LINE messages to any group.

Flexible rewards

Points, stamps, or online claims. Staff-verified in-store or instant digital codes. Birthday bonuses, milestone rewards, and win-back campaigns included.

Automated messages

Set up a welcome message for new joiners, a win-back campaign for inactive members, and birthday rewards — all running automatically without lifting a finger.

Live analytics

See which members are active, which rewards are popular, and how your automations are performing. No spreadsheets required.

Gamification

Daily scratch cards and lucky spin wheels with configurable win rates — keeping members engaged between visits.
